The Strategy



The DREAMER

You start with a position where dreaming takes place without limitations and restrictions. Create a great vision regardless of any limits to your imagination.



The REALIST

The next position is the one of the realist who is the implementation strategist or in other words, the realizer. Here the big picture is broken down into individual work steps, individual bites and actively plans about the implementation. The big question is: How to make the dream come true.



The CRITIC

The last position is the critic. His job is it (as in a kind of quality control), to detect weak points in the realization and find out why it can't work. He finds the points that are problematic and checks everything critically.
